This page is your guide to various websites and products of Data Resources and Statistics division.

Data Portal

This data portal contains macro-economic data about member and non-member countries. Make your own vizualizations. This portal is maintained by Knoema.

Statistical Yearbook 2017

Check most important statistical highlights and trends covering more than 200 social, economic and financial indicators for all the 57 IDB member countries.

Country Trend lines

Check trends of our member countries covering most important macro economic indicators sources from latest IMP, World Bank and Economist Intelligene Unit. You can download both Excel and PDF.

IDB Group Operational Data Dashboard

Explore the development projects financed by the Islamic Development Bank Group since its inception in the year 1395 Hijri (1975) through this interactive dashboard.

IDB Group Operations aggregated tables

IDB Group approvals since inception organized by Mode of Financing, Entity, Source of Funds, Country and Sectors.The IDB Group Operational Data is released quarterly based on the Gregorian calendar; however, for local use the Operational Data is also presented in the Hijra year.

Build Aggregates on the fly

This interactive site allows you to choose indicators from the World Bank's World Development Indicator as well as a selected weight and calculate on the fly IDB aggregates.

ERPD Intranet Site

Check the latest research papers and publications of the Economic Research and Policy Department, including statistical publications.

This is an Intranet site. You need VPN access if attempting to view from outside IDBHQ.

SDG Data Dashboard of IDB Member Countries

This interactive dashboard allows you to monitor the progress of our Member Countries on SDG indicators. The data is sourced from UN Statistical Division's website.
